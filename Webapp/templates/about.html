Earthquake Prediction System

About Earthquakes

Earthquakes are caused by sudden movements of the Earth's tectonic plates. Seismic waves travel through the ground, leading to surface shaking.

They are classified based on magnitude: small (less than 4.0), moderate (4.0 - 6.0), and major (above 6.0).

Prediction Models

Scientists use different models to predict earthquakes:

  • Machine Learning: Uses historical earthquake data to detect patterns.
  • Seismic Hazard Mapping: Identifies high-risk zones based on past events.
  • Deep Learning AI: Uses neural networks to analyze seismic wave patterns.
